## 2. Technical Architecture & Implementation

### A. Layer 1 — Persistent Confidence Engine (EWMA Observer)

Created in [temporalStateEstimator.cjs](file:///c:/Dev/iRacing-Companion/desktop/telemetry/temporalStateEstimator.cjs), this layer tracks rolling confidence levels $C_t$ for all key detectors:
$$C_t = \alpha C_{t-1} + (1 - \alpha) E_t$$
We defined category-specific decay and persistence parameters ($\alpha_{\text{aero}} = 0.85$, $\alpha_{\text{slip}} = 0.70$, $\alpha_{\text{chassis}} = 0.80$, $\alpha_{\text{hybrid}} = 0.75$). This allows physical slip phenomena to decay rapidly while aeromechanical packers and ride-height collapses remain persistent.

### B. Layer 2 — Multi-Detector Fusion Engine

Aggregates independent persisted confidence streams to isolate coupled non-linear vehicle instabilities:

- **Coupled Aero Instability ($I_{\text{aero}}$)**: Correlates pitch oscillation and diffuser stall.
  $$I_{\text{aero}} = 0.4 \cdot C_{\text{heave}} + 0.4 \cdot C_{\text{stall}} + 0.2 \cdot (C_{\text{heave}} \cdot C_{\text{stall}})$$
- **Coupled Entry Instability ($I_{\text{entry}}$)**: Correlates brake migration snap and axle over-rotation.
- **Coupled Traction Instability ($I_{\text{traction}}$)**: Correlates wheelspin slip and exit torque asymmetry.

### C. Layer 3 — Subsystem Health Models

Maintains rolling health indexes ($0.0 \to 1.0$) reflecting the mechanical degradation of the car:

- **Aero Platform Health**: Decays under severe grounding and coupled aero instability ($\beta = 0.95$).
- **Rear Stability Health**: Decays under wheelspin and coupled traction asymmetry ($\beta = 0.90$).
- **Brake Migration Stability**: Decays under lockups and entry snap slides ($\beta = 0.92$).
- **Hybrid Deployment Integrity**: Decays under exit deployment surges ($\beta = 0.95$).

### D. Layer 4 — Hypothesis Engine (Recursive Bayesian Latent Fault Inference)

Estimates latent physical conditions using recursive Bayes updates:
$$P(F_t \mid z_t) = \frac{P(z_t \mid F) P(F_{t-1})}{P(z_t \mid F) P(F_{t-1}) + P(z_t \mid \neg F) (1 - P(F_{t-1}))}$$
We track four hypotheses:

1. **Floor Damage Probability**: Accumulates under sustained grounding, non-reversible (very slow decay). Triggers `FLOOR_DAMAGE_ALERT` if $P \ge 0.80$.
2. **Diffuser Choking Probability**: Accumulates under diffuser stalls, dynamic (decays immediately when speed drops).
3. **Tire Degradation Likelihood**: Accumulates under axle slip energy, non-reversible stint wear. Triggers `TIRE_DEGRADATION_ALERT` if $P \ge 0.75$.
4. **Brake Overheating Likelihood**: Accumulates under locks, slowly cooling down over time.
