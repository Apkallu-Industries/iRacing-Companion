### B. Speed-Dependent Dynamic Aero Confidence Curve
Implemented in the `createTelemetryEvent` factory in [eventDetector.cjs](file:///c:/Dev/iRacing-Companion/desktop/telemetry/eventDetector.cjs). When an aeromechanical event is triggered, the engine evaluates real-time vehicle speed against the platform's minimum aero threshold (`minAeroSpeedKph`):
$$\text{AeroConfidence} = \text{BaseConfidence} \times \left(0.20 + 0.80 \times \left(\frac{\text{speed}}{\text{aeroMinSpeed}}\right)^2\right)$$
This decays the confidence exponentially down to 20% as speed approaches zero, accurately reflecting that aerodynamic groundings and platform stalls have lower physical plausibility at slow speeds.

### C. Advanced Prototype-Specific Detectors
Designed and deployed 4 high-fidelity detectors inside [eventDetector.cjs](file:///c:/Dev/iRacing-Companion/desktop/telemetry/eventDetector.cjs):
1. **Aero Platform Oscillation Detector (`AeroPlatformOscillationDetector` v1.0.0)**: Catches vertical heave/damper resonance (porpoising) when high-speed (>160 km/h) front damper deflection velocities are out-of-phase:
   * Event: `AERO_PLATFORM_OSCILLATION` (observed)
2. **Diffuser Underbody Stall Detector (`DiffuserStallDetector` v1.0.0)**: Flags flow detachment under high dynamic pressure where rear ride height collapses below 18mm under acceleration:
   * Event: `DIFFUSER_STALL` (observed)
3. **Brake Migration Instability Detector (`BrakeMigrationRotationDetector` v1.0.0)**: Catches corner entry over-rotation instability for non-ABS platforms under heavy braking when the oversteer index is high:
   * Event: `BRAKE_MIGRATION_ROTATION` (interpreted)
4. **Hybrid Exit Torque Surge Detector (`HybridTorqueAsymmetryDetector` v1.0.0)**: Monitors hybrid exits for drivetrain asymmetry when throttle > 60% and left/right driven wheels display a slip speed delta > 4.5 m/s:
   * Event: `HYBRID_DEPLOYMENT_SURGE` (observed)
